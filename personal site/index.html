About me

I'm a passionate educator, tutor, and mentor with a lifelong dedication to helping students succeed. With experience in diverse teaching environments—from classrooms to one-on-one tutoring—I specialize in literacy, language learning, and personalized education strategies.

Barton Tutoring

The Barton Reading & Spelling System is a structured, Orton-Gillingham-based program designed to help individuals with dyslexia and other reading difficulties improve their literacy skills. It is a systematic, explicit, and multisensory phonics-based program that teaches spelling, reading, and writing in a step-by-step manner.

ESL

At Seabright Sand, we specialize in teaching English as a Second Language (ESL) to learners of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're just starting out or looking to improve your speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills, we’re here to support you every step of the way.
